About Dana Marino Design

Dana Marino Design offers custom stationery and graphic design services for weddings, special occasions,
personal and business branding and stationery. We'll work comprehensively to ensure you are
fully involved in the creative process, together achieving an end result that surpasses expectations.
Services include full wedding and special event invitation suite design, 'day of' stationery design
including menus, programs, table numbers and escort cards, welcome tote bags, event signs and more.
