Dana Marino is a graphic designer based out of New Jersey. Graduating from Ramapo College of New Jersey with a B.A. in Design and Interactive Media, Dana is well-versed in all areas of the field and specializes in design for print.


In working at a top global brand licensing agency, Dana has gained corporate experience in the areas of branding, web design, style guide development, and creation of marketing collateral.


Dana Marino Design offers custom design services for weddings, events, businesses, personal stationery, and more. I work comprehensively with all clients to make sure they are fully involved in the creative process, together achieving an end result that surpasses expectations.
