PORTFOLIO • WINE PACKAGING DESIGN

The client looked to extend a well-known beer brand into different product categories such as wine. As the company's main logo and branding is focused on the theme of magic, I translated this to the idea of a circus, a whimsical place that is seemingly magical.


For the three labels I kept the central graphic of an abstract circus tent the same, only changing the colors per different varietal for a cohesive look. The illustrations and typography differentiate each label and correspond to the varietal name.
