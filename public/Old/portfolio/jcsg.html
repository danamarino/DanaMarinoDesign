PORTFOLIO • JULIEN CHUNG WEB DESIGN

Julien Chung, an award-winning graphic designer and illustrator, recruited Bradford Design Group for the creation of an online style guide organizing his numerous licensing program assets and designs. As lead designer on the project, I developed a layout that could be easily programmed for web.


Organizing Julien's designs into concise collections for ease of licensee usage was top priority. The layout is simple, easy to navigate, and showcases Julien's bright and bold artwork.
