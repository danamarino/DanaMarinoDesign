PORTFOLIO • BEAUCOURANT BRANDING + WEB

Beaucourant was seeking a strong identity to position itself in the fashion industry. For the client, I developed a full corporate identity package for a cohesive presence across all mediums.


Beaucourant features vintage styles with a modern edge - illustrated in the brand’s name which combines the French “Beau” (beautiful) with “au courant” (aware of what is going on; fashionable). Beaucourant's logo is a mix of contemporary typography and organic vine illustration.


The look and feel of Beaucourant’s website directly reflects the brand’s unique identity. The organized layout is user-friendly, with clear navigation and purpose. Each item for sale is showcased in order to highlight the product, and surrounded by vintage textures for a multi-dimensional look. Beaucourant’s online store is supplemented with an interactive look book, which provides customers with the season’s hottest trends.
