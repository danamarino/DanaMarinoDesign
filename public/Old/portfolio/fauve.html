PORTFOLIO • FAUVE PAINT BROCHURE

The paint collection Fauve, inspired by artist Henri Matisse, required collateral for consumers to take away from the retailer. Based on the Fauvism movement led by Matisse - signified by strong color and bold brush strokes - I created a brochure that was not only informative, but also emulated the inspiration behind the brand.


The cover design incorporates a combination of hand drawn illustration for a rough brush stroke effect and simple typography for a contemporary feel. The unique die-cut edge is inspired by Matisse's La Gerbe.


The interior of the booklet showcases the paint swatches along with Matisse quotes and relevant photography to show real-life application of the color. The abstract La Gerbe shapes continue throughout the brochure for a cohesive look.
