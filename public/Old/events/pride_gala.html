EVENTS • THE PRIDE GALA

The Pride Gala, an annual fundraising event to benefit Immaculate Conception High School, celebrated its second year with the theme "As we continue to reach for the stars."


I worked to create an invitation suite to embody this theme while maintaining the school's traditional navy blue and ivory colors. With falling stars and a formal script, The Pride Gala invitation gave guests a first look at the elegant evening to come.
