EVENTS • MICHELLE'S BRIDAL SHOWER

With vintage glamour serving as the inspiration for Michelle's Bridal Shower, I utilized blush pink and peach tones for a simple but classy invitation. Instead of using the traditional cards provided by retailers for registry information, I created a custom registry card to match.


For a seamless look, I carried the vintage style throughout the event with "Advice for the Bride" signage, candy station labels, and personalized treat bags for guests to take home.
