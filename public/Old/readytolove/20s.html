WEDDINGS • READY TO LOVE • ROARING 20'S

For Michelle and Michael's Marco Island destination wedding, we wanted to capture a chic and romantic feeling... straying from a typical casual beach wedding vibe. After deciding on a color palette, we created a Save the Date postcard with a custom drawn Marco Island map to give guests the first feeling of a fabulous getaway.


For the invitation suite we created a classic meets contemporary style, incorporating traditional letterpress printing with hits of silver foil for shine. The cool greys and touches of fuschia reflected the couple's modern taste, which was continued throughout the event's floral and decorative details.


Upon checking into the gorgeous Marriott Beach Resort, guests received a custom designed "Plan to Get a Tan" welcome bag, complete with a welcome booklet and Florida-themed goodies. During the ceremony, guests were relieved from the hot Florida sun with our custom fan-style programs, while at the reception the tables were finished with elegantly styled table names and menus.
