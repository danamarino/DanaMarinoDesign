WEDDINGS • CUSTOM DESIGN • NICOLE + MICHAEL

For Nicole and Michael's rustic chic wedding, I wanted to capture the style of the event and add a special personal touch for the couple. Looking into the venue, The Inn at Millrace Pond, the shape of the sign on The Inn's door captured my attention. With custom illustration and lettering accents, I incorporated details unique to The Inn to give Nicole and Michael a truly personalized Save the Date.


I carried a sense of simplicity through the invitation suite, using a ribbon effect on the couple's names to tie them together. Enclosed in a rustic mocha tone pocket sealed with lace, the whole package is a glimpse of the couple's romantic day to come.
