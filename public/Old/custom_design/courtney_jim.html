WEDDINGS • CUSTOM DESIGN • COURTNEY + JIM

As owner of Fabulously Chic Weddings, professional wedding planner Courtney had a clear vision in mind for every aspect of her big day. I was happy to actualize Courtney's vision throughout her stationery using bold black and white with a touch of luxe gold.


For the invitation suite, I combined graphic black and white stripes with a subtle modern script, highlighting a custom monogram for Courtney and Jim. Enclosed in a black pocket, the invitation suite previewed the classic glamour of the event to come.


Bold table numbers popped against the soft ivory sequin tablecloths and gorgeous floral arrangements, and black and white event signage completed the overall look of modern elegance for Courtney and Jim's special day.
