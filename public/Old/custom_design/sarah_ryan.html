WEDDINGS • CUSTOM DESIGN • SARAH + RYAN

For Sarah and Ryan's Fourth of July wedding in Naples, the couple wanted to give guests a first look at the fantastic events planned throughout the weekend. They decided on a unique all-in-one postcard format for their Save the Date, in which guests could easily tear off a "Pre-RSVP" for an early count of guests attending.


I tied the rich colors of Sarah and Ryan's bold engagement photography into a color palette of deep red, navy, and charcoal. As Sarah and Ryan were likely not utilizing the Fourth of July theme throughout their actual wedding decor, we decided this was a fun way to incorporate the Americana theme into their special event.
