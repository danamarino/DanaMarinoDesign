One of my favorite events to design stationery for is a wedding. I believe in the importance of a beautiful invitation suite to give guests a first glimpse of your special day. I work with my clients closely from the very beginning, initiating the design process by learning about your vision for the whole event. I create with you... ensuring that the final results exceed your expectations.


Services include custom design of save the dates, invitation suites, monograms, escort cards, menus, programs, event signage, welcome bags, tags + labels, and more! I work with reputable print shops that specialize in letterpress, foil, thermography, digital and offset printing to create the finished pieces.


In addition to Custom Design, I also offer a selection of designs that are Ready to Love. These styles can be customized with your favorite wording, colors, typography, and more.
